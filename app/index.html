Alien Invader Defender

Welcome to Alien Invader!!
The point of this game is to place and upgrade towers and their designated spots and prevent the aliens from reaching the end of the path and removing your health.
After reading the rest of the instructions hit your Space Bar to start the game.

Pay attention to your health and EXP points. It takes 50 points to place a tower and to make your first upgrade. After that each upgrade is 50 points multiplied by the tower level.

Every 8th level delivers a slow moving but hard to kill boss that will reduce your health more than a regular alien.

Upgrading and placing towers is as simple as clicking on the black squares on the board. I suggest starting off with the top right square for your first tower.
